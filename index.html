DSM Andre Pectin

DSM Andre Pectin is a 75% joint venture between Royal DSM, a global nutrition, health and sustainable lifestyle research company, and Shandong Andre Group Co. Ltd., a leading manufacturer of fruit juices.

DSM acquired the original stake in Andre Pectin in 2013, and in 2019 increased DSM's total stake in the company to 75%. DSM Andre Pectin is located in Yantai City, Shandong Province, China. The company, founded in 2005, is Asia's largest producer of apple and citrus pectin, hydrocolloids, offering texture solutions for food, beverage and personal care products, and has first-class access to the world's fastest growing specialty food ingredients market.

Together with DSM's controlling interest in DSM Zhongken Biotechnology Co., Ltd. and DSM Rainbow (Inner Mongolia) Biotechnology Co. Ltd., DSM has a strong portfolio of hydrocolloid products with gellan gum, xanthan gum, vellan gum and pectin-based solutions that DSM offers products in the food, beverage and personal care markets.

DSM Andre Pectin is a longtime member of the International Pectin Producers Association (IPPA).

DSM RAINBOW BIOTECHNOLOGY is a high-tech enterprise focusing on the research and production of application innovations of microbial polysaccharides and other biological gums. We provide better biogum solutions and higher quality services to our customers. Our gellan gum, welan gum and new welan gum have obtained the National Invention Patent, the products quality and research level play a world-leading role in biogums and hydrocolloids field.

The gellan gum and xanthan gum produced by Rbbio company are widely used in food, soy products, dairy products, suspending pulp juices, candy, medical capsules and the household products. Industrial grade xanthan gum, welan gum and new welan gum are used in oil drilling mud ,casing cement , construction cement, dry-mixed mortar and concrete admixture with the superior performance and stable quality. Our products are well accepted by customers which have a broad market prospect and strong domestic and international market competitiveness .
